Common Concerns

We Hear These All the Time

Totally fair. Here's an honest answer to each one.

📘

"I already have a Facebook page"

Facebook is a great tool, but you're renting space on someone else's platform. They control who sees your posts, they change the rules constantly, and not everyone is on Facebook. A website is yours. No algorithm decides whether your hours show up. No platform can shut you down. And when someone Googles your business name, a real website is what they expect to find.

🤝

"My customers already know me"

That's genuinely wonderful, and word of mouth is powerful. But your current customers aren't your only potential customers. Every person who moves to the area, every visitor passing through, every person a friend recommends you to, they all do what everyone does now: they Google you. If nothing comes up, some of them won't bother to dig further.

💸

"It's too expensive"

This one surprises most people. A simple, professional website starts at $200. Hosting runs $10 a month, which is less than most people spend on coffee in a week. Compare that to what you're probably spending on flyers, ads, or listings on other directories. A website works for you every single day, around the clock, without costing anything extra.

🤯

"I wouldn't even know where to start"

That's exactly what we're here for. You don't need to know anything about how websites work. We handle everything: the design, the build, getting it online, even the photography if you need it. You show up to a coffee meeting and tell us about your business. We take it from there.
