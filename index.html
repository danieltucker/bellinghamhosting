FAQ

Good Questions

Honest answers about how this all works.

What kind of sites do you host?

Static sites only — think HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and image files. No WordPress, no databases, no server-side code. If your site is simple and fast, it's a great fit.

What's your uptime like?

Honest answer: pretty good, but not guaranteed. This is a small operation run by one person on solar-powered hardware. Servers need updates, hardware occasionally needs attention, and things happen. I'll always let you know in advance when I can, and I'll get things back up as fast as possible. If you need five-nines uptime, a big cloud provider is probably a better fit.

What happens if you go on vacation?

Sites keep running — they don't need me around to stay up. If something breaks while I'm out, I'll respond as soon as I'm back. I'm transparent about this tradeoff and the price reflects it.

Can I move my site away if I want to?

Absolutely. Your files are always yours. I'll hand everything over with no hassle if you ever want to move on.
