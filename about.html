Background

Enterprise Experience, Local Focus

Before Bellingham Hosting, I worked on large-scale web projects for some of the biggest names in tech. That experience taught me how to build things that hold up — fast, clean, and built to last — without the enterprise price tag.

These days I take what I learned in those big environments and apply it to the small businesses that actually need it. A local restaurant or family-owned shop shouldn't get a worse website than a Fortune 500. They just need it at a price that makes sense.
