Services covered

These terms apply to web hosting, web design and development, photography, and ongoing site update services provided by Bellingham Hosting.

Web hosting

  • Hosting is billed monthly via Stripe. Plans are Business ($10/mo) and Non-Profit ($5/mo).
  • Hosting is provided on a best-effort basis. Uptime is not guaranteed. This is a small, solar-powered operation — planned and unplanned outages may occur. If guaranteed uptime is required, a large cloud provider is a better fit and we'll tell you so.
  • Hosting covers static websites only (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images). Sites requiring server-side processing, databases, or e-commerce backends are outside scope unless separately agreed upon in writing.
  • You may cancel hosting at any time. Unused days in a billing period are not refunded.
  • If a hosting plan lapses, we'll hold your files for 30 days before removal.

Web design and development

  • Projects begin with a free consultation. A written scope of work is agreed upon before any paid work starts.
  • Payment terms: 50% deposit before work begins, 50% upon project completion and approval.
  • You are responsible for providing all content (text, photos, logos) in a timely manner. Delays in content delivery may delay your launch date.

  • Upon final payment, you own your website files outright. We will provide all source files.
  • Refunds: if you cancel after work has begun, you forfeit the deposit. If we are unable to complete the agreed scope, a prorated refund will be issued.

Ongoing updates
